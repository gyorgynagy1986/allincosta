Business Services

-Autonomo and Company service setting -

main steps to set up autonomo

Your Business. Seamlessly Established.

Starting a business in Spain should be exciting, not overwhelming.

At ALLinCOSTA, we simplify every step — whether you’re becoming an Autónomo (self-employed) or creating a Spanish company (SL) by our qualified partners. Our multilingual partner team manages documentation, registrations, and compliance, so you can focus on your vision.

Ideal for freelancers, consultants and independent professionals.

01

Consultation & Planning

We assess your activity, income goals, and residency status to choose the best structure.

02

NIE & Social Security Registration

Assistance in obtaining your tax identification (NIE) and enrolling in Seguridad Social.

03

Hacienda (Tax Office) Registration

Set up your business activity (IAE) and tax obligations (IVA / IRPF).

04

Digital Certificate Setup

We handle your Certificado Digital to access all Spanish e-services easily.

05

Ongoing Support

Bookkeeping, invoicing templates, tax declarations, and compliance monitoring.

From idea to success ALLinCOSTA is by your site.

main steps to set up Company (Sociedad Limitada – SL)

Start your business with confidence with ALLinCOSTA.

Ideal for investors, entrepreneurs and established professionals.

01

Legal Name Reservation & NIE for Directors

Verification and registration with the Central Mercantile Registry.

02

Bank Account & Share Capital Deposit

We guide you through Spanish banking procedures.

03

Notary & Company Incorporation

Preparation of statutes and official signing with a notary.

04

CIF & Tax Registration

Your business receives its corporate tax number and fiscal setup.

05

Social Security, Licenses & Bookkeeping Setup

Full operational start — ready to trade confidently.
