Life in the Community

- Living in Spain is more than paperwork, it’s about feeling at home -

Main Steps to Set Up Your Life in the Community

Settle in with Confidence and Connection.

Our “Life in the Community” service helps you by our qualified partners to integrate, connect and enjoy your Costa del Sol lifestyle from day one within your community.

01

Legal Constitution

The community is officially created once the first owners register their properties and the building has its title deed.

02

First General Meeting

Owners meet to approve community rules, elect the President, and appoint the Administrator.

03

Opening Community Bank Account

A dedicated account is opened for shared expenses, maintenance, and insurance payments.

04

Budget & Fees Agreement

The annual budget and individual owner contributions (quotas) are agreed upon and documented.

05

Insurance & Service Contracts

Essential services — cleaning, maintenance, security, gardening, and insurance — are contracted and coordinated.

06

Ongoing Management & Communication

Regular meetings, transparent updates, and efficient administration keep your community well-managed and compliant.

Services for the Community

+

1. Service for the President by ALLinCOSTA

+
  • Find your ideal Administrator and service providers for your community.
  • Create a guide for your home owners to settle their life smoothly and stress free.
  • Support to set up ideal communication channels between the board, owners and administrator.
  • Install the right model to follow up service action (daily, weekly, monthly track).
  • Advice to launch a benefit package for home owners.

2. Service for the Homeowners by the Administrator

+
  • Residency & TIE cards – renewals and family applications.
  • Health Insurance & Medical Registration – private and public systems.
  • Utilities & Internet Setup – electricity, water, gas, phone, Wi-Fi.
  • School & Childcare Guidance – international and bilingual options.
  • Local Registration (Empadronamiento) and municipal services.
  • Community and Property Management communication support.

We make your transition smooth, personal and worry-free, because life here should be effortless in harmony.
