Business Services

Your Business, Seamlessly Established

Starting a business in Spain should be exciting, not overwhelming.

At ALLinCOSTA, we simplify every step — whether you're becoming an Autónomo (self-employed) or creating a Spanish company (SL).

Our multilingual partner team manages documentation, registrations, and compliance, so you can focus on your vision.

Autónomo Setup – Step by Step

Ideal for freelancers, consultants, and independent professionals.

1.Consultation & Planning

We assess your activity, income goals, and residency status to choose the best structure.

2.NIE & Social Security Registration

Assistance in obtaining your tax identification (NIE) and enrolling in Seguridad Social.

3.Hacienda (Tax Office) Registration

Set up your business activity (IAE) and tax obligations (IVA / IRPF).

4.Digital Certificate Setup

We handle your Certificado Digital to access all Spanish e-services easily.

5.Ongoing Support

Bookkeeping, invoicing templates, tax declarations, and compliance monitoring.

Company (Sociedad Limitada – SL) Setup

For investors, entrepreneurs, and established professionals.

1.Legal Name Reservation & NIE for Directors

Verification and registration with the Central Mercantile Registry.

2.Bank Account & Share Capital Deposit

We guide you through Spanish banking procedures.

3.Notary & Company Incorporation

Preparation of statutes and official signing with a notary.

4.CIF & Tax Registration

Your business receives its corporate tax number and fiscal setup.

5.Social Security, Licenses & Bookkeeping Setup

Full operational start — ready to trade confidently.

QUALIFIED PARTNER OF ALLinCOSTA handles the paperwork. You focus on your growth.
