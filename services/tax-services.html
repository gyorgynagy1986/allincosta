Tax Services

FOR NON-RESIDENT OWNERS & RENTAL INCOME, AUTONOMO

Spain’s tax system can be complex — especially for non-residents and foreign property owners.

Our certified partner tax advisor(s) ensure compliance, transparency, and peace of mind, while helping you optimize your tax position.

Main steps to apply for tax services in Spain

Your Taxes Simplified. Professionally Managed.

Determine Your Tax Residency

  • You are considered a tax resident in Spain if you spend more than 183 days in Spain in a calendar year or if Spain is your main economic interest.
  • If you are a non-resident, different rules apply.
01

Profile & Property Review

We identify your tax status — non-resident, Autónomo, or company — and review your property and rental details.

02

Tax Identification (NIE & CIF)

We ensure you have the correct tax ID (NIE for individuals, CIF for companies) for all declarations.

03

Income & Expense Collection

We gather rental income records, invoices, and deductible expenses to calculate your tax base accurately.

04

Tax Calculation & Optimization

Our experts apply the correct rates, exemptions, and deductions to minimize your tax liability.

05

Quarterly & Annual Declarations

We prepare and file all required non-resident, rental, and VAT returns — on time and fully compliant.

06

Ongoing Fiscal Guidance

Receive continuous support, updates on tax law changes, and representation before Spanish authorities when needed.

Your peace of mind begins with precise tax care, which is our priority from your first investment to long-term ownership.
