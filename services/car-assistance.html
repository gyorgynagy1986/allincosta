Car Assistance

- Buying, selling, renting your car -

Main steps to register your car

Drive your dream, we handle the rest.

Our qualified partners review all requested and collected car papers (registration, purchase invoice, conformity certificate, etc.) to ensure everything is ready.

01

Technical Inspection (ITV)

The vehicle must pass the Spanish roadworthiness test. We arrange and accompany you through the process.

02

Customs & Tax Procedures

We handle import duties, registration tax, and road tax (where applicable).

03

Official Translation & Administration

All required documents are translated and submitted to the Spanish Traffic Department (DGT).

04

Registration & Plates

Once approved, your Spanish registration number is issued — and we organize your new license plates for pick up.

05

Final Delivery

You receive your car fully legalized, insured, and ready to drive in Spain — hassle-free!

Required documents & expected costs

+

Required Documents

+

1. Registration Document (Log Book)

2. Identification

Address authorization:

  • Escritura Notarial de la Vivienda (Notarial Property Deed) or
  • Contrato de Alquiler (Rental Contract) or
  • Empadronamiento (Padron)

3. If the Owner Changes in Spain

  • Contrato de Compraventa (Sales Contract) or
  • Factura (Invoice)

Expenses (Gastos)

+

1. Impuesto de Matriculación / Registration Tax

0–14.75% of the vehicle value – depending on brand, model, year, and CO₂ emissions.

2. Impuesto de Transmisiones / Transfer Tax

4% or 8% – only if the owner changes.

3. Various Fixed Expenses

586.00 € (Includes: reduced file, Traffic fees, Plates, Agency fees, fiscal label registration, courier, environmental badge...)

4. ITV / MOT

Approx. 155.00 €

5. Impuesto de Circulación / Road Tax

Between 42.00 € and 144.00 € annually (in Estepona).

Customs (Non-EU Vehicles Only)

+

Only for vehicles outside the EEC:

  • 10% Customs Duty (Arancel)
  • 21% VAT (IVA)
  • Approx. 300.00 € Customs Agency (Agencia de Aduana)

Change of Residence (Tax Exemption)

+

You can save on Registration Tax if you meet these requirements:

  • The car must be in your name for at least 6 months
  • Certificate of consular deregistration (Certificado de baja consular), dated within the last 30 days
  • Original purchase invoice (Factura original de compra)
