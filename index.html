What is an NIE / NIF?

The NIE (Número de Identidad de Extranjero) is a foreigner individual identification number required for legal and financial transactions in Spain, including property purchases, bank accounts and tax filings.

The NIF (Número de Identificación Fiscal) is the tax identification number issued for foreign companies for legal and financial transactions in Spain, including property purchases, bank accounts and tax filings.

ALLinCOSTA assists clients in obtaining their NIE/NIF quickly and correctly through our qualified partner service.

What are Non-Residence Taxes?

Non-resident property owners in Spain are subject to annual non-residence taxes, which vary depending on whether the property is rented or vacant. These taxes are based on property value and other factors.

What is IBI?

IBI (Impuesto sobre Bienes Inmuebles) is the annual property tax paid to the local municipality. It is based on the property’s cadastral value and is typically paid once per year. ALLinCOSTA can arrange payment and ensure deadlines are met by our qualified partner service.

What is a POA (Power of Attorney) in Spain?

A POA allows someone to act on your behalf for specific legal or administrative matters, such as signing property documents or managing financial affairs. ALLinCOSTA can coordinate the process and ensure the POA meets Spanish legal requirements. POA also can be prepared in your residence country at the Notar office.

How does the car registration process work in Spain?

The car registration process in Spain requires paperwork, inspections (ITV) and payment of fees and taxes. ALLinCOSTA offers a full car registration service, including paperwork preparation, appointments and delivery of registration documents by our qualified partner service. The process is online, only one personal visit to collect the new original car documents and presents on ITV which will be organized by our partner as well .

What is PPC?

PPC (Permiso de Primera Construcción) refers to a construction permit or official approval for property works or renovations. ALLinCOSTA guides clients through the application and approval process with our partner service.

What is a Deed?

A deed (escritura) is the official legal document proving ownership of a property. It is signed at the notary and registered in the Land Registry. ALLinCOSTA coordinates notary appointments and ensures your deed process is smooth.

What is a First Occupancy License?

A First Occupancy License (Licencia de Primera Ocupación) certifies that a property meets all building regulations and is safe for habitation. It is required before moving into the property.

Can the reservation fee for a property be refunded?

Reservation fees vary by property and agreement. In most cases, reservation fees are refundable only under certain conditions, such as failure to obtain a mortgage or issues with legal compliance.
