Welcome to ALLinCOSTA!

- Your gateway to effortless luxury living in Costa del Sol -

Relocating or having a 2nd home in a new country is an exciting journey, but it comes with its challenges. We exactly know what you mean and how you feel and how much info and how much contact, how much time do you need to be settled as we all went through without any info and support for 4 years ago.

At ALLinCOSTA, we turn challenges into seamless experiences, helping you transition into your dream life in Costa del Sol with ease, confidence, and sophistication.

As the founder of ALLinCOSTA with 30+ years of international corporate business experience across multiple industries and countries with success and proven results, I understand the power of strong relationships, partnerships, personal branding, and exceptional service. Having lived abroad for over 15 years in different countries and now calling Costa del Sol home for the past 3.5 years (since 2022), I have personally navigated every aspect of relocation from legal procedures to home setup, banking, insurance, car registration, legal and taxes and community integration.

Starting from scratch, I went through every step firsthand securing NIE, finding the perfect property, handling legal formalities, overseeing interior designing process, managing utilities, finding the right insurance services and even tackling local tax and car registration complexities.

Moreover, in an unexpected way, my dedication to excellence and high standards with professionalism led me to be nominated and more times elected president of a prestigious 143-apartment international community, where I built a unique, luxury network and an elite-quality organized lifestyle.

Now, I am here to share my knowledge, connections and experience with you, to save your time, energy and unnecessary stress. Whether you are moving, investing or simply seeking a high-quality life on Costa del Sol,

ALLinCOSTA ensures a smooth, luxurious and worry-free transition all in.

Your dream life awaits, so why do you wait?

Let's make it happen!

WITH LOVE,

Bernadette

International Business Expert & CEO
