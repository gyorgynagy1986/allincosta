Mission – ALLinCOSTA

Your Dream, Our Expertise

We help international clients and expats who are seeking a new home, investment, or fresh start on the Costa del Sol. Whether it’s real estate, legal and tax services, starting a business, or lifestyle support — we provide it all in one place, with trusted and qualified local partners.

You dream it – we make it happen.

Vision – ALLinCOSTA

A New Standard of Living on the Costa del Sol

Our vision is for ALLinCOSTA to become the region’s NUMBER 1 premier premium service provider — where relocating, investing, or starting anew is not just a process, but an experience.

A place where the client is the true value, and where trust, expertise, and personal attention come together seamlessly. A one-stop solution that turns dreams into safe realities — stress-free, reliable, and exceptional.

My name is Bernadette Varhelyi as the founder of ALLinCOSTA with 28 years of international business experience across multiple industries and countries with success and proven results. I understand the power of strong relationships, partnerships, personal branding, and exceptional service. Having lived abroad for over 15 years, and now calling Costa del Sol home for the past 2.5 years, I have personally navigated every aspect of relocation from legal procedures to home setup, banking, insurance and community integration.

Starting from scratch, I went through every step firsthand securing NIE, finding the perfect property, handling legal formalities, overseeing interior designing process, managing utilities, and even tackling local tax and car registration complexities. My dedication to excellence and high standards led me to be elected president of a prestigious 143-apartment international community, where I built a unique, luxury network and an elite-quality lifestyle.

Now, I am here to share my knowledge, connections and experience with you, to save your time, energy, and unnecessary stress. Whether you are moving, investing, or simply seeking a high-quality life in Costa del Sol, ALLinCOSTA ensures a smooth, luxurious, and worry-free transition all in.

Your dream life awaits, so why do you wait? Let's make it happen!
