Full-service property assistance

- Buying, selling, renting your property -

Whether you’re planning to relocate, invest, or find a second home in Costa del Sol, buying property abroad can be a complex and overwhelming process. That’s where ALLinCOSTA comes in. From NIE application to signing the deed, we provide full assistance throughout the entire buying, selling, or renting journey. We handle every detail—so you can enjoy a smooth, stress-free experience while we take care of the paperwork, coordination, and local requirements. We work closely with trusted legal partners, real estate agents, and local authorities to make sure your property transaction is safe, efficient, and tailored to your needs.

PROPERTY PURCHASE ASSISTANCE

Navigating the real estate market in Costa del Sol can be daunting, but with our expert guidance, the process becomes seamless. We offer comprehensive support from finding the perfect property to signing the deeds, ensuring a stress-free experience for our clients.

The main steps process to purchasing in Spain:

  • Define Your Goals - Is this a holiday home, investment, or permanent residence?
  • Secure Financing - Get pre-approved for a mortgage (or plan your funds).
  • Property Selection - Access exclusive listings and visit handpicked properties.
  • Due Diligence - Work with top lawyers to verify legalities and contracts.
  • Purchase Process - Make an offer, sign the reservation contract, and pay the deposit.
  • Final Steps & Ownership - Close the deal, handle taxes, and get the keys to your dream home!

NIE Application Support

Obtaining your NIE (foreigner identification number) is a crucial step for anyone looking to live or invest in Spain. Our team will assist you in the application process, making sure all the necessary documentation is in order and helping you navigate any potential hurdles along the way.
